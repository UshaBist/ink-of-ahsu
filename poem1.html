I Wish I Could Paste My Heart Over There
Where fondness was in the aroma of air,
Offer bountiful blossoming flower to dear,
& exchange serenity with each drop of tear.

I wish I hadn't known, beats aren't mine,
Spraying fragrance over fetid ain't divine,
Blossoming bleeding heart contains vine,
Serenity is not soulful, it will take off shine.
