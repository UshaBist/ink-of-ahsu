A LETTER TO MY SON

I was 20 when i conceived you and held your for the first
My mewling baby, you were more than enough to quench my thirst
Holding you felt like, grasping the whole world
You were molten liquid, and i turned to be your mould

I kept my ambitions and urges aside, to raise you
I kept ownself in gloom, to lighten your future and to praise you
I chose to live in fetor so that i could offer you aroma
I cuddled all the sorrows so that i could surround you with just chroma

I still remember those golden days, when i hadn't turned gray
And that fortunate time, when i used to be included in your pray
Remembrance of your giggles are yet enough to make my day

But.. my questionnnnn
My question remains still unanswered

I failed sprinkling etiquettes on you is the cause for whatever happened today??

I could have huddled up, If i was occupying more space over there
I could have shut my voice, If everything i used to utter were proven ill-fated there

Tell me..tell me
Where did i go wrong dearie?
Nothing was i doing except blessing you merely...
What should i do with this empty lap now?
Whom shall i refer this old heart to?

I am sorry, my dear son
But, somethings i feel like ,If you were not grown up...
you would be my little kid fluttering, vibing and thriving around me filling my life with your joys

But how could a mother be mean?
I am happy...happy with your decision
But i am afraid too
That your old age will be soon knocking at your doorsüö™
And you..you may have to write a letter like this.
