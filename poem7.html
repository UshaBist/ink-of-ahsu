Unnamed

I love the beauty flowers carry with themselves.I love how they're differently similar.Every part of them has significant meaning. And people have different choices in flowers.Some go for thorny yet the elegant roses, some go for loyalty of sunflower towards sun, some go for fragrance of jasmine and some for endless layers of marigold. Meanwhile i have a unique choice in flowers.In a beautiful garden of flowers I've fallen for that underrated flower yellowish in colour smiling to me, curing all my anxieties.It looks so delicate that even a light touch would tear it apart from its delicate stem. Its irony that neither do i know its name nor its family yet the softest corner of my heart always chooses it over all the other flowers in the garden.I feel happier to see it dance in the rhythm of nature.People roaming around with curiosity making different faces, stare at me every single day.Some even manages to ask me about it.And i go numb as always.Because neither do i have any name to address it with and nor do i have significant reasons to adore it with eyes full of love. It attracted me effortlessly and i have no specific reasons to justify my nurture for it. Why just adoration isn't enough? Why do i need to name it? Aren't my eyes full of love enough to differentiate it among all the other flowers in the garden.It feels so terrible to mould my way of expressing in accordance to the people around. Now that i am afraid of looking at you because i still got no name to provide you.So, i choose to remain silent and secretly gaze at you making sneak peeks .May be this is why people keep unnamed things a mystery.As It is lovely to feel and terrible to explain.
